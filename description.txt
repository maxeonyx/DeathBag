Mediumcore quality-of-life: on death, your items go into a persistent bag instead of scattering on the ground. Walk up to your death bag to restore your inventory to exactly how it was, slot positions and all.

Features:
- Death bags preserve your full inventory layout (slot positions, stacks, armor, accessories, loadouts)
- Walk near your death bag — it magnets toward you and auto-restores
- Bags persist in the world across sessions
- Teammates can see your bag, pick it up, and deliver it to you
- Multiple bags supported (die twice, get two bags)
- Loadout Station: craftable furniture that snapshots your current inventory into a portable loadout bag
- Loadout bags can be dropped anywhere as a saved gear set — right-click to restore

Designed for co-op Mediumcore play. Pair with Panic at Dawn and Boss Hexes for the full experience.
