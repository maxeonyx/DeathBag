Mediumcore quality-of-life: on death, your items go into a persistent bag instead of scattering on the ground. Right-click to restore your inventory to exactly how it was, slot positions and all.

Features:
- Death bags preserve your full inventory layout (slot positions, stacks)
- Right-click your bag to restore — current items are re-absorbed via normal stacking
- Bags persist in the world even across sessions
- Other players can see your bag but can't take from it
- Multiple bags supported (die twice, get two bags)

Designed for co-op Mediumcore play. Pair with Panic at Dawn and Boss Hexes for the full experience.
